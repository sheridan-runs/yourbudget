Startup Finance FAQ

Is my data safe on YourBudget?

Yes. YourBudget is a local-first application. We do not use a database. All financial data is stored in your browser's Local Storage and never leaves your device.

What is Zero Cash Date?

Zero Cash Date is the projected calendar date when your company's cash balance will reach $0.00 based on your current Net Burn Rate.

How do you calculate Net Burn Rate?

Net Burn Rate = (Cash at Start of Month) - (Cash at End of Month). It represents the total cash decrease over a specific period.

How much runway does a startup need?

Most investors recommend maintaining 18-24 months of runway after a funding round. Having less than 6 months of runway is considered critical risk.
